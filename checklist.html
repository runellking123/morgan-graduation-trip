Planning Checklist

Sorted by urgency. Red = book ASAP, Gold = book 3–4 weeks before, Blue = 1–2 weeks before or day-of.

  • Book Sprinter van rental — Call all 5 Houston companies, compare rates, book the best option. Confirm: out-of-state approval, mileage policy, insurance, pickup/return times. Add both Runell & Marvin as authorized drivers. Book by mid-May.
  • Book Airbnb / vacation rental — Use the listings on the Travel & budget page and search Airbnb/VRBO as needed. Aim for a 4+ bedroom pool home within 20 min of the convention center. Book for 3 nights (Thu–Sun). Book by mid-May (June is peak season).
  • Contact Capella Commencement Office — Confirm guest ticket count (need 7 for group of 8 minus Morgan). Ask about additional tickets or overflow viewing. Confirm regalia/cap & gown status. Call by early June.
  • Make celebration dinner reservation — Call Shooters Waterfront or your chosen restaurant to reserve a table for 8 on Saturday, June 27, around 7:00 PM. Mention it's a graduation celebration. Book 3–4 weeks ahead.
  • Book Sunday activity — If choosing Jungle Queen Riverboat, book tickets online at junglequeen.com. If Xtreme Action Park, check for group deals at xtremeactionpark.com. Book 2–3 weeks ahead.
  • Purchase graduation celebration supplies — "Dr. Morgan King" banner/sign, navy & gold balloons, flowers, card, small gifts. Order custom items online by early June.
  • Plan outfits for graduation — Semi-formal/dressy casual for guests. Coordinate navy/gold/white. Comfortable shoes. Lightweight fabrics for Florida heat. Have backup outfit for kids.
  • Confirm van inspection & roadside assistance — Ask the rental company about their vehicle inspection process. Confirm spare tire, jack, and roadside assistance coverage for the 2,200-mile round trip.
  • Pack road trip essentials — Cooler with food & drinks, chargers, aux cable, pillows/blankets, kid entertainment (tablets, coloring books, games), first-aid kit, sunscreen, umbrella (June = rain season).
  • Download offline maps — Download Google Maps offline for the full route (Houston → Fort Lauderdale). Cell service can be spotty through rural Mississippi/Alabama. Download entertainment for kids.
  • Grocery run for Airbnb — Stop at a Publix or Walmart after arriving in Fort Lauderdale Thursday night or Friday morning. Buy breakfast supplies, snacks, drinks, and any pool/beach supplies needed.
  • Charge all devices & cameras — Ensure cameras, phones, and portable chargers are fully charged for graduation day. Bring extra memory cards.
  • Confirm all reservations 48 hours before departure — Call/message to confirm: van pickup time, Airbnb check-in instructions, dinner reservation, activity bookings.
  • Cook & prep road trip breakfast — Night before departure (Wed June 24): cook a full breakfast to eat Thursday morning at home before 5 AM departure. Prep cooler with road food.
